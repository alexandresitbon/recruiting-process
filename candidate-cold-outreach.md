## Message subject for any role

- Option 1: Are you ready for something harder?
- Option 2: Ready for the hardest problem you've worked on?
- Option 3: Is your current role still exciting?
- Option 4: Are you playing it too safe?
- Option 5: What's the most ambitious thing you could do next?

##  Message for product managers

Hey [First Name]!

Most fintech is built for knowledge workers who sit at desks. We're building for the HVAC tech who's under a crawlspace and needs to expense a part in 10 seconds — or the fleet manager trying to stop fuel fraud across 40 drivers without a finance team.  
  
I'm the SVP of Product at Coast, and I've been following your work on [X]. The way you approach [specific thing] is exactly the kind of thinking we need as we scale our product for the trades.  
  
Would you be open to a quick conversation? No pressure — I just think what we're working on would be interesting to you.

Talk soon,
Alexandre

##  Message for product designers

That's the core tension we're solving at Coast — fleet cards, expense management, and spend controls built for the trades. I lead product here and I've been looking for designers who care about users that most of fintech ignores.  
  
Your work on [X] caught my attention. Worth a 20-minute call?

Talk soon,
Alexandre

## Personalization notes

- [ ] Reference a specific piece of work or project you genuinely admired
- [ ] Mention the role level that matches their experience
- [ ] About the role for Head of Design: This is a hands-on role where you'd set the design bar at Coast while staying close to the work
- [ ] About the role for Senior / Staff Product Designer: This is a role where you'd own design end-to-end on one of our product squads with real autonomy and a high bar for quality, business impact, and delight.
- [ ] About the role for Senior / Staff Product Manager: This is a high-ownership role where you'd drive the strategy and execution of a product area, working closely with design and engineering from conception through development and rollout.
