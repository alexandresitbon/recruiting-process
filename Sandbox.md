## Interview questions
- Explain a time you managed conflict.
- Explain a time you learned a valuable lesson.
- Tell me about a time you had to adapt.
- Tell me about a time where you made the wrong decision.
- Tell me about a time when you made a mistake
- Tell me about a time you solved a pain point for customers.
- Tell me about a time when you raised the bar.
- how do you consider the impact of your work on the world?
- tell me about a time you made a decision based on your instincts
- tell me about your proudest project
- [Leadership] Tell me about a time you coached or mentored someone more junior. What did you work on with them, and how did they grow?
- [Culture] Tell me about a time you actively shaped the culture of a team you were on — beyond just doing your job well.
- [Culture] Tell me about your involvement in hiring at a past company. What role did you play, and what did you learn from it?
- [Agency] Tell us about a problem you identified at work that wasn't yours to solve. What did you do about it?
- [Ownership] Tell us about a time you stayed involved in something well past what your role technically required.
- [Process & Momentum] Tell me about a project that was losing steam. What did you do to get it moving again?
- [Process & Momentum] Walk me through a time you had to manage your own direction on a project without much guidance. How did you decide what to do next?
- [Quality] Tell me about a time you realized engineers weren't using the design system components you'd built or expected them to use. What did you do about it?
- [Growth & Self Reflection] Tell me about a hard situation—something really challenging that you went through in the last year or two... If you could go back and change anything, what would you do differently?
- [Trade-Offs] Tell us about a time you had to make a decision to make short-term sacrifices for long-term gains.
- [Ethics] Tell me about a time you pushed back on a decision — from a manager, a PM, or a stakeholder — that you thought was wrong. What happened?
- [Impact] Think of a long-term project in your portfolio. If you had two more months to work on it, what would you have done differently? What would have you added or continued to refine?
- [Feedback & Conflict] Tell me about a time you had a real disagreement with a PM or engineer about a design decision. What happened, and how did it get resolved?
